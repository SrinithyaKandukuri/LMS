KEY FEATURES OF OUR LIBARY

Digital Cataloging

We Will Establish a robust digital cataloging system that includes metadata for each book, such as title, author, publication date, subject, and keywords.

File Organization

We Will Organize digital book files in a logical and consistent manner. And We are Having a facility of folder structure based on categories, genres, or subject areas, making it easy for users to navigate and locate books.

Backup and Data Redundancy

We Are Having Regular backup procedures to protect the digital book collection from data loss And The Main Thing In Our LMS Is maintaining redundant copies of the books in separate storage locations or on cloud-based platforms .

Digital Rights Management

If our e-library includes copyrighted material, consider implementing DRM mechanisms to protect intellectual property rights. DRM can restrict unauthorized copying, printing, or sharing of e-books.
